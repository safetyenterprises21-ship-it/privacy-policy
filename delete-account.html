Delete Your Safety Enterprises Account

If you wish to delete your Safety Enterprises Distributor account and the associated personal data, you may request account deletion by contacting us.

How to Request Deletion

Please send an email with the subject: Account Deletion Request

Email: safetyenterprises@gmail.com

Please include:

  • Shop Name
  • Owner Name
  • Registered Mobile Number

What Happens After Your Request

  • Your account will be permanently disabled.
  • Your profile information will be removed from our active database.
  • Your future access to the app will be revoked.

Data That May Be Retained

Certain records such as invoices, completed orders, or information required by applicable laws may be retained for legal, accounting, tax, or regulatory purposes.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are normally processed within 7 business days.
